AI Methodology & Logic
📡

Real-Time AQI Data

Fetches live European AQI from the Open-Meteo Air Quality API using precise lat/lng per Hanoi district. No API key required, CORS-enabled.

📊

Hourly Time-Series

24-hour chart with past hours at full opacity, future hours faded. Current hour marked with dashed vertical indicator labeled "NOW".

🧮

Exposure Risk Model

Risk = (AQI/500) × 100 × Sensitivity × (Hours/8). Tiers: Low ≤15, Moderate ≤35, High ≤60, Severe >60.

🎯

Safe Window Optimization

Sliding-window scans all consecutive 2-hour pairs across the 24h timeline and returns the minimum average AQI window.
